Mamma Mia Macaroni

(See other recipes!)

Description

Mamma Mia, here we go again!

Hide this from your lactose intolerant friends and your ABBA haters :)

Ingredients

  • 4 cups macaroni
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2.5 cups flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 lb cheese

Recipe

  1. Cook the macaroni while still firm
  2. Beat the egg in a bowl. Add butter and flour
  3. Whisk that bowl at medium heat, pour into macaroni and stir
  4. Bake at 350 F for 20-25 minutes
