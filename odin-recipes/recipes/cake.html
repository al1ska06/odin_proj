Cheri Cherry Cobbler

(See other recipes!)

Ingredients

  • As many cherries as humanly possible
  • Stick of butter
  • Cup of flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • Salt, vanilla, and almond extract to taste

Recipe

  1. Melt the butter
  2. Whisk together butter and all dry batter ingredients
  3. Add cherries to baking pan, pour batter above
  4. Bake at 350 F for 55-60 minutes
