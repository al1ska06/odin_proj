Fairytale Frappe

(See other recipes!)

Description

I'm in love, with a fairyta-

With the caffeine boost from this easy recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 shots cold espresso
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 oz ice cubes
  • whipped cream

Recipe

  1. Add milk, extract, and ice cubes into blender
  2. Blend on low and then increase to high to encorporate air
  3. Pour this into a glass, pour espresso above it
